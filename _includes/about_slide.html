UNIHACK is a hackathon run for university students, by university students in Melbourne, Australia.

Teams have 24 hours to design and build something awesome. It can be anything - a website, a mobile application, a video game, or even something hardware. The important thing is that it needs to work when it is presented to the judges.

UNIHACK was created by Terence Huynh and Matthew Rossi. It is being organised with the help of WIRED Monash, the IT student society at Monash University Clayton.
